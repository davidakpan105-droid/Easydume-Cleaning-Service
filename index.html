FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Easy Dump & Cleaning Services.

What areas in Akwa Ibom do you cover?
We serve all areas within Uyo and the wider Akwa Ibom State, including residential estates, commercial districts, hospitals, hotels, and government facilities.
Do you offer 24/7 waste collection?
Yes. Easy Dump operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We offer emergency waste collection and cleaning services to ensure your facility is always clean and compliant.
How do I book a service?
Book through our online form, call 0911 434 0770 or 0810 901 7974, email info@easydump.ng, or send a WhatsApp message. We respond within hours.
Is your fumigation safe for children and pets?
Our fumigation team uses certified, regulated chemicals following strict safety protocols. We advise clients on re-entry times and offer low-odour, child-safe options.
Do you offer cleaning staff outsourcing?
Yes. We provide trained, vetted cleaning personnel on a contract basis with full onboarding, supervision, and performance management so you focus on your business.
How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
For emergencies, we respond within 1–2 hours in Uyo and surrounding areas. Our 24/7 operations team is always on standby.
